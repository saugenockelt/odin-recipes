Spaghetti Recipe

Introduction

Spaghetti are the backbone of the italian cuisine, without spaghetti there is basically nothing left

Every child loves getting entangeled in spaghetti and splattered by delicious sauce!

ingredients

  • 500g of Spaghetti
  • a sauce

Steps

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Pasta
  3. boil for approximately 8 minutes
  4. pour off the water
  5. serve with the sauce
  6. Mamma mia!

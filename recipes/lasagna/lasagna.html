Lasagna

Instructions

To achieve the worlds best lasagna you must mix all ingredients and put it into the oven!

The exact time and heat doesn't really matter, just go with your flow!

Buon Apetito!

ingredients

  • 500g of Tomatoes
  • 500g of grounded Beef
  • 300g of finest buffalo mozarella
  • mucho amore!

Steps

  1. Add the Tomatoes, the grounded Beef and the buffalo mozarella into a bowl
  2. Turn on your oven
  3. Wait approximately 40 minutes
  4. Enjoy!
